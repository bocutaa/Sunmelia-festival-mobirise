Sunmelia Experience

Art and Inspiration

While music is an important part of Sunmelia, art is also a core element. There are multiple venues including a circus, theater, museum quarter and much more. Artists and performers come from all over the world to display their talents. There are also opportunities for people and artists to work together in order to create memorable and meaningful artwork.

Green Sunmelia

At Sunmelia, we deeply care about environmental sustainability and development. We try and convey this message to people in an effort to create a more aware and conscious community. Every year we work on becoming greener and take measures towards achieving such goals.

Discover Our City: Cluj

While once you enter Sunmelia you may never want to leave, we recommend checking out Cluj as well! There are a ton of things to do from sight-seeing and checking out some of the most amazing bathhouses, to eating out at great restaurants, bar hopping between ruin pubs, and dancing through the night at bars and clubs... the possibilities are endless.

Our Cause: Smiley

Sunmelia partnered with Smiley this year, to support the program’s initiative on spreading music to children coming from underprivileged backgrounds. Music is a powerful tool that can help children improve their focus, motivation, creativity and confidence, while allowing them to overcome socio-economic boundaries. Every reusable cup dropped off at a collection point at Sunmelia, went towards supporting the Smiley program.

Accommodation

On-site camping options
Basic camping

Camp for FREE! With your 6-Day Pass, 3-Day Pass or day tickets (at least for two consecutive days) you can camp at Sziget Festival at the designated basic camping zones, almost anywhere, except program venues and closed upgrade campings.
FOR FREE

Siesta Camping

Siesta during Fiesta! Fantastic shady camping spots, extra services and a cool vibe - Siesta Camping provides you all of these. Super-nice area at the Western part of the island is awaiting everyone looking for a little rest. Not to mention that the Sziget Beach is really close!

Park Camping

If you don't want to walk too far from the entrance with your luggage, the west side located Park Camping is definitely the place for you. No need to worry about the blazing sun in the morning, as this camping is full of trees.

Sunmelia Ville

The South located Sziget Ville is the is the home of all our glamping accommodations. A great option if you don't want to bother with pitching your own tent. Try out our newbies like The Rose, The Who, The Boss or Igloo Box.

Tents, houses, special options

ibis Hotels

Are you dreaming of a vibrant and comfortable stay? At ibis hotels, full of passion for music, you can charge you batteries and re-energise for the rest of the festival!

Bell Tent

Te(n)mpting accommodation! Not everyone wants to pitch and pack away their own tent and hassle of lugging it. Do you recognize yourself? Then the Bell tent is built for you. A leisurely and comfy festival experience.
