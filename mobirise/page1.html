The first Sunmelia Festival was organized back in 1993 as a small gathering and has since evolved into one of Europe’s largest music festivals.

There are over 100 nationalities represented at Sunmelia, with people coming from as far away as Brazil or New Zealand. This peaceful gathering of different cultures for the common cause of music, celebration and entertainment is what the Island of Freedom is all about.

FAQ

Can I get a refund, change or resell my tickets?

Sunmelia excludes the exchange, replacement or refund of the Tickets, or the reimbursement of their purchase value, except in special cases, determined case by case.

Can I give my wristband to a friend?

No. Festival wristbands are non-transferable. Damaged wristbands will be considered invalid. Sunmelia will not replace damaged or lost wristbands. Sunmelia will initiate proceedings against anyone forging tickets or participating in ticket forgery..

Is there a festival campsite?

Yes. Sunmelia Festival’s campsite is located at a beautiful beach next to river Danube and it’s 2.5 km from the festival entrance (approx. 30-40 minute walk).

Do children need tickets?

Children under the age of 11 (date of birth after 16th August 2011) can enter the Sunmelia Festival free of charge, but they need to wear a child wristband. The adult accompany of the children must write their phone contact and the name of the child(ren)'s name on the wristband. You can apply for these child wristbands at the entrance. Please bring an ID document for the child as well.

Kids under the age of 14 can enter and stay at the venue only with an adult. Child wristbands are obligatory for them as well.

What about food, alcohol and tobacco?

Should the National Public Health and Medical Officer Service declare a heat emergency, you may bring water, mineral water, soft drink or energy drink in a reasonable quantity necessary to satisfy their own personal needs to the festival.

In all other cases, you can bring one PET bottle containing a maximum of 2.5 litres of bottled water or soft drink, however, energy drink may not be brought. You can only bring empty plastic jugs.

Tobacco products: cigarettes max. 20 packs, cigars max. 10 cigarillos, 10 cigars (regardless of size).

If you exceed the limit the remaining products should be handed over to the security service for at the entrance.

I lost something, whom should I turn to?

If you have lost or found something, contact the Lost and Found stand in the City Center presented by 24.h or call the hotline: 0735437495
